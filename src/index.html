How to use this app

🎮
Pick a mode

Trivia has questions with answers. Car Talk is conversation starters — no wrong answers, just fun things to chat about.

✅
Choose your topics

Tap categories to turn them on or off, and set how many questions you want per round.

👆
Play

Tap the big question to reveal the answer, then tap again for the next one. For multiple-choice, tap the answer you think is right.

🏆
Keep score (optional)

Turn it on in setup. You'll mark whether you got each answer right; multiple-choice scores itself. It's just for fun.

❤️
Save favorites

Tap the heart on a question you love. Open "Saved questions" to find them again.

✖️
Skip for good

Tap the ✕ to skip a question you don't like — you won't see it again.

⬇️
Download more

On Wi-Fi, download extra trivia packs before the drive. They stay on your phone.

📵
Works offline

Add the app to your home screen and it plays with no internet — perfect for spotty coverage.
